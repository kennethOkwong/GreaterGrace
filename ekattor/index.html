About

Welcome to Greater Grace Schools

Greater Grace Schools (GGS) is a top private primary/secondary school in Uyo, Akwa Ibom State. It serves pupils and students in creche to primary 5 and JSS1 to SS3 respectively. The school is manage by the proprietor. Dr. Dolf Akaninyene The mascot is the Knight, and the school colors are scarlet and gold.
